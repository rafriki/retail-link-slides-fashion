Craig Vidler
Chairman

Craig Vidler, Chairman of Retail Link and Group Managing Director of HR GO plc, has more than 40 years’ experience in the recruitment sector.

After early success in retail, in which he became buying and merchandising controller for a department store group by the age of 24, he used his considerable expertise to place senior people in the industry.

He built Star Executives into the UK’s largest retail recruitment company, finding talented people for board and other positions in leading retail groups. Craig placed managing directors with Selfridges and Harvey Nichols.

He has made a vast contribution to the recruitment sector, understanding, inspiring and mentoring his teams to share his passion for the business and total professionalism at all times.

For 14 years, Craig was a Director and Trustee of Retail Trust, the industry charity. In 27 years, his annual fly fishing days for leading figures from retail and other sectors have raised more than £220,000 net.

His achievements are featured in industry magazine Recruitment International and the March 2015 edition of Kent Life.
